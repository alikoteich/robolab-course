What is an Input?
Sends data to the Arduino

An input is anything that gives the Arduino information about the world. Sensors and buttons are inputs — they detect something (light, distance, a press) and report it as an electrical signal the Arduino can read.

💡
Think of inputs as the robot's sensesPush button, light sensor, ultrasonic sensor → all inputs
📤
What is an Output?
Receives commands from the Arduino

An output is anything the Arduino controls to make something happen in the real world. LEDs, buzzers, and motors are outputs — the Arduino sends them a signal and they respond with light, sound, or movement.

⚡
Think of outputs as the robot's actionsLED, buzzer, servo motor → all outputs
